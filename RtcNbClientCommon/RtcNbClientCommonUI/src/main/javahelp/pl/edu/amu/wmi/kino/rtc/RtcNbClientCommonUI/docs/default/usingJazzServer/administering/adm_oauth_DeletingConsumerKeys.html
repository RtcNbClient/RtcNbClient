Deleting consumer keys

You can delete authorized consumer keys to discontinue communication between two servers.
Before you begin
You must be a member of the JazzAdmins group.
About this task
To delete an authorized consumer key:
  1. Click the Server tab.
  2. In the Configuration pane, click OAuth Consumer Management .
  3. On the OAuth Consumer Management page, in the Authorized Key section, beside the key that you want to delete, click the Delete Consumer Key icon.
  4. Confirm that you want to delete the key. After the key is deleted, the key is removed from the Authorized Keys section of the page.
