Renaming and reassigning a plan

Important: Plans modifying plans is not available in Netbeans plugin for Rational Team Concert. Following information relates to web user interface.

You can rename a plan or reassign a plan to a different project area or team area. You can also reassign a plan to a different iteration.

To configure a plan

  1. In the Web UI, click Plans .
  2. To configure a plan, hover the mouse pointer over a plan and click . If you want to delete a plan, click . The progress bar displays the progress of the plan.

    • Change the team area, timeline, iteration, and plan type of the plan.
  4. Click Save .
